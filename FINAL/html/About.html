ABOUT

My personal film and media journal.
This is where I track everymovie, TV series, animation, and short film I've watched, rate them, and share my thoughts.
It's a place where I can reflect on what I've seen and keep a running list of what I plan to watch next.
In addition to my own entries, ScreenDiary allows others to comment on the films and shows I've reviewed, sharing their own thoughts and experiences.
If the viewers think I'm missing out on something great, there's also a suggestion box where viewers can recommend new titles for me to explore.
It's a space for me to document my cinematic journey and engage with others who share the same passion.
